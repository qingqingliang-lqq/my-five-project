About Space Survivors

Space Survivors is an exciting space-themed survival game where you navigate through dangerous environments while battling alien enemies. Pilot your spaceship through asteroid fields and enemy territories, collecting resources to upgrade your ship and weapons.

The game features a unique blend of arcade action and survival elements, challenging you to last as long as possible in the hostile depths of space. With each survival run, you'll unlock new ship components and weapons to enhance your chances in future attempts.

How to Play

  • Movement: Use WASD or arrow keys to navigate your spaceship.
  • Shooting: Click or press spacebar to fire your weapons.
  • Special Abilities: Press Q, E, or other designated keys to activate special abilities.
  • Collecting: Fly over resources to collect them automatically.
  • Upgrading: Access the upgrade menu when prompted to enhance your ship.
