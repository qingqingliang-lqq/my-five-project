About Dungeon Survival

Dungeon Survival is an exciting game where you explore dangerous dungeons filled with monsters and traps. Collect items, fight enemies, and try to escape with your life!

As you delve deeper into the dungeons, you'll encounter increasingly challenging foes and find powerful weapons and upgrades to aid you in your quest.

How to Play

  • Movement: Use WASD or arrow keys to navigate through the dungeon.
  • Attack: Click to attack enemies when in range.
  • Collect Items: Walk over items to collect them automatically.
  • Upgrade: Access the upgrade menu when prompted to enhance your character.
  • Survive: Use strategy to avoid traps and defeat enemies.

Game Features

Dungeon Survival offers a variety of features that make each playthrough exciting:

  • Multiple character classes with unique abilities
  • Variety of enemies with different attack patterns
  • Challenging puzzles and traps to overcome
  • Loot system that rewards exploration

Tips and Strategies

To maximize your chances of survival, consider these strategies:

  • Keep your health in check and find health potions when needed.
  • Use ranged attacks against enemies to keep your distance.
  • Explore thoroughly to find hidden treasures and power-ups.
