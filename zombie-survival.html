Zombie Survival

Fight against hordes of zombies in this thrilling survival game!

About Zombie Survival

Zombie Survival is an action-packed game where you face endless waves of zombies. Your mission is to survive as long as possible while defeating the undead and collecting weapons and power-ups to enhance your character.

As you progress, you'll face increasingly difficult zombies and need to use strategy and quick reflexes to stay alive. Collect resources, upgrade your weapons, and see how long you can last in this zombie apocalypse!

How to Play

  • Movement: Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character.
  • Aim: Use your mouse to aim your weapon.
  • Shoot: Click to fire your weapon at zombies.
  • Reload: Press R to reload your weapon when needed.
  • Switch Weapons: Use the number keys or scroll wheel to switch between weapons.
  • Collect Items: Walk over items to collect them automatically.

Game Features

Zombie Survival offers a variety of features that make each playthrough exciting:

  • Multiple weapons with different damage and fire rates
  • Various types of zombies with unique abilities
  • Power-ups that enhance your character's abilities
  • Progressive difficulty that increases over time
  • Detailed scoring system to track your performance
  • Fast-paced action that tests your reflexes

Tips and Strategies

To maximize your survival time in Zombie Survival, consider these helpful tips:

  • Always keep moving to avoid being surrounded by zombies
  • Conserve ammunition by aiming carefully and going for headshots
  • Prioritize upgrading your favorite weapons
  • Use obstacles in the environment to funnel zombies and control crowds
  • Save special weapons and power-ups for when you're in trouble
